A maan entred who could hardly hav been less than six feett six inhes in hight, with the chest and limbs of a Hercules. 
His dress was rich with a richnes which would, in England, be looked upon as akinn to bad taste.
Hevay bands of astrakhan were slased acros the sleves and fronts of his double-breasted coat, while the deap blue clok which was thrown over his sholders was lined with flame-colored silk and secured at the neck with a brooch which consisted of a single flamming beryl. 
Boots wich extendd halfway up his calves, and which were trimed at the tops with rich broun fur, completed the impreson of barbaric opulance which was sugested by his hole apearance.
